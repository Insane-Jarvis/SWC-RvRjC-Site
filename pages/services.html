Our Services

After securing admission at the Institute and starting your stay here, you may feel that a lot of parameters around you are different. You would have more responsibilities to handle at the hostel and at the academic level. Take heart, you will not be the only one. There are a few issues that almost everyone in the Institute faces initially like academic concerns, social (family and peer) pressure etc., and leading to feelings of loneliness and anxiety that impacts personal morale and academic performance.

It is important to understand that students may lose focus and subject to these pressures. If not attended to at the right time, this could lead to poor performance -- both academically and personally.

To help you refrain from losing focus and being unhappy, your Counsellor encourages you to approach for any problem that you are facing- be it academic, emotional, social or financial- without hesitation.

Counselling provides an opportunity for individuals to learn to make better choices, improve interpersonal skills, develop confidence and increase educational effectiveness.

Our Service Offerings

Individual Counselling

The Student Wellness Centre provides individual counselling to help students address a wide range of personal and academic challenges.

  • Students get a safe space to talk without pressure or judgment
  • Confidentiality is strictly maintained
  • Counsellors respect students and their problems
  • Counsellors help students consider different options and make choices
  • Counsellors make an attempt to reduce the problem and provide support
  • Counsellors can intervene with the right people to help students if required

Appointments are available from 10.00 am to 4.00 pm Monday-Saturday, and students are usually seen for 40 minutes each depending on demand. Students can take an appointment by visiting the following link:

Psychological Assessment Tests

In some cases where more precise diagnoses are required, the Wellness Centre conducts psychological assessment tests to help identify and address specific needs.

Workshops and Events

The Wellness Centre organizes various workshops and events during the academic year, focusing on topics related to personality development, mental health, and student needs, such as emotional intelligence, self-confidence, stress management, and time management.

The center also organizes events on occasions like Mental Health Day, International Happiness Day, and World Suicide Prevention Day, to create awareness about the importance of timely interventions with a counsellor for quicker problem-solving.
